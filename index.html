Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

We use enterprise-safe tools and best practices; you'll get a data-handling checklist and policy template.

How technical do I need to be?

No coding required; optional low-code labs included.

Will this help my career or business?

You'll complete a capstone and earn a certificate with practical proof of impact. This isn't just theory; you'll build real-world projects that demonstrate your skills to employers or clients. The certificate validates your expertise and gives you tangible evidence of your capabilities.

What is the time commitment?

4–6 hours/week for 6 weeks, live + async, designed for full-time professionals.

Can you tailor for our industry?

Yes! We can definitely design a tailored curriculum to your industry whether it's finance, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, and more.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is required! We start from the basics and gradually build up to advanced concepts. The course is designed for beginners who are motivated to learn and professionals looking to upskill.

How does the access work?

You have two flexible options:

  • Lifetime Access: Pay once and own it forever. Perfect if you want to learn at your own pace and never worry about access expiring.
  • Yearly Unlimited: Subscribe annually for full access. Great if you prefer lower upfront costs or want to complete the material within a year.

Both options include the same complete content, certificate, and community access. You can switch between plans or cancel anytime.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

We cannot offer a refund after the 14-day trial. Users must cancel before getting charged. If you're not satisfied with the course for any reason, you can cancel your membership; no questions asked.

Will I get support if I get stuck?

You'll have access to our private community forum where instructors and fellow members can help. We also offer live and personalized Q&A sessions to address your specific questions.

Can I switch from yearly to lifetime?

Yes! If you start with a yearly subscription and decide you want lifetime access, you can upgrade at any time. We'll credit your remaining subscription time toward the lifetime price.

What kind of certificate will I receive?

You'll receive a verified digital certificate that you can share on LinkedIn, add to your resume, or showcase to employers. It includes the course name, your name, completion date, and a unique verification ID that anyone can verify by scanning a a QR code.

Are there any discounts for teams?

Yes! We offer special pricing for teams and organizations. Contact us at team@aimbootcamp.com for group rates and customized enterprise solutions.

What happens after I complete the program?

You'll join our alumni network with access to continued learning opportunities and exclusive resources. We also offer advanced courses where alumni get special discounts.
